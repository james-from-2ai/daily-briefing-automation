AI mental health in LMICs: first causal RDD evidence from Brazil

A April 2026 medRxiv preprint from Brazil (Saude Mental Digital CBT chatbot) reports a fuzzy regression discontinuity design measuring the causal impact of an AI-powered CBT chatbot deployed in primary care on anxiety and depressive symptoms among registered patients aged 18+.
