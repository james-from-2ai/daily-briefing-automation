The AI Weather Quest 2026 benchmarking competition held its MAM (March-April-May) Awards webinar on June 18, spotlighting best-in-class sub-seasonal ML forecasting. Sub-seasonal forecasts (2-6 weeks out) are directly relevant to anticipatory action and crisis preparedness — the planning window before an extreme weather event where early intervention is still possible.
