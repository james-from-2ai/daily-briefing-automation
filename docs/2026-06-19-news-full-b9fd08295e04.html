AI in global health (LMIC) — EVAH call for proposals now open

The EVAH initiative ($60M from Gates Foundation, Novo Nordisk Foundation, and Wellcome) has opened its call for proposals for locally led evaluations of AI health tools in LMICs. The initiative targets rigorous evidence on which AI tools work in primary care, where they add value, and how they can be deployed responsibly. Wellcome's Charlotte Watts: "We want to ensure everyone, everywhere can benefit." Alongside EVAH, India's Ministry of Health launched the SAHI strategy and BODH benchmarking platform for health AI in February 2026, both positioning India as an evidence-generation hub. EVAH press release

So what for 2AI: EVAH is the most direct funding opportunity for 2AI's existing health evaluation work — the call criteria map cleanly onto Kenya CHW support and maternal health pilots. This is separate from (and potentially stackable with) the OpenAI People-First fund.

AI governance in Africa — South Africa policy drama + AU strategy

South Africa's 2026 draft National AI Policy was withdrawn in April 2026 after it emerged that the document included AI-hallucinated citations in its reference list — an embarrassing incident for government credibility on AI. More constructively, the African Union's Continental AI Strategy Phase I (2025–2026) is focused on national governance frameworks, with an OECD-Kenya workshop held in Nairobi in March 2026 to build capacity. SA AI Policy wiki · OECD AU Governance report

So what for 2AI: The SA policy hallucination incident is a cautionary tale for LMIC governments adopting AI for policy — and an argument for 2AI's role in evidence-based AI governance support. The AU strategy is a potential door-opener for government partnerships in East Africa.

AI fintech and financial inclusion — India's UPI + LMIC credit gap

India's UPI processed 2,264 crore transactions worth ₹29.53 lakh crore in March 2026 alone — at a scale that makes it the world's largest AI-enabled payment infrastructure. DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) has cumulatively transferred ₹49 lakh crore, saving ₹4.31 lakh crore by algorithmically purging ghost beneficiary entries. In parallel, MSME AI lending is addressing an estimated $5.2T global financing gap by underwriting on mobile/transaction data instead of credit history. Kenya's M-Pesa has added real-time fraud detection AI and personalized agronomic-financial advisory. India AI financial inclusion

So what for 2AI: AI + financial inclusion is increasingly adjacent to 2AI's agricultural advisory work (farmer access to credit + advisory in the same app). The DBT accuracy gains are an analogue for what 2AI measures in CHW support — worth citing in cost-effectiveness work.

AI in humanitarian response — scaling IRC Signpost + UNHCR strategy

The IRC is scaling its Signpost AI chatbot platform across Bangladesh, Nigeria, and new geographies — displaced people can access food, legal documentation, and education referrals in local languages, without internet access in some modes. UNHCR published its AI Approach framework (September 2025, now operational), covering refugee status determination, interview transcription, and UNHCR-WFP data integration. The emphasis is on human-rights-anchored AI, not just efficiency. Rest of World on IRC Signpost

So what for 2AI: IRC Signpost is the closest operational parallel to what 2AI's Turn.io-based deployments could become. The human-rights-anchored framework UNHCR is building is also the accountability architecture 2AI will need for any government-partnered health deployment.
